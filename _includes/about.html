## About me

As a PhD student at Karolinska Institutet in the Department of Physiology and Pharmacology, my research journey, which began in August 2019, is dedicated to unraveling the complexities of molecular pain. Specializing in neuroimmunology and innate immunity, my work is particularly focused on chronic pain, a field that merges my academic interests and research pursuits.

Currently based in the Biomedicum at Karolinska Institutet, my research centers on macrophages in the dorsal root ganglia and their role in chronic pain conditions.
